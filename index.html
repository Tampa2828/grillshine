About Grill Shine

Grill Shine is a locally owned & operated owner-operator service founded on 08/14/2025. We specialize in professional, eco-friendly BBQ grill cleaning across the Greater Tampa Bay area—Tampa, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Brandon, Wesley Chapel, and nearby communities.

Using high-temperature steam, we remove grease, residue, and bacteria without harsh chemicals—right at your home. Our deep cleaning process restores your grill’s performance, improves food taste, and extends its life.
