Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our eco-friendly BBQ grill cleaning service.

Get a Quote Call (813) 555-0199
What is the process?

We arrive at the scheduled time, protect surrounding surfaces, and clean your grill in its current location using high-temperature steam.

What areas do you serve?

Greater Tampa Bay, including St. Petersburg and Clearwater.

How long does BBQ grill cleaning take?

Typically 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on the grill’s condition and size.

What do I need to do before cleaning?

Please make sure the grill is in working order and that we have access to your outdoor living space, a water faucet, and an electrical outlet.

Do I need to be home?

No, you don’t have to be home. We’ll just need to agree on payment arrangements prior to the appointment.
